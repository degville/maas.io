About MAAS

Metal as a Service brings the language of the cloud to physical servers. It makes it easy to set up the hardware on which to deploy any service that needs to scale up and down dynamically; a cloud being just one example.

What is MAAS

The cloud is full of virtual machines and sometimes a virtual machine is to virtual. Sometime real work requires real machines. Setting up hardware can take a long time and you have to chose between the flexibility of the cloud and raw power of real metal. That is where MAAS comes in.

MAAS delivers real servers on demand. So you can give a few servers to MAAS then spin them up on demand just like a cloud. MAAS handles all the boring stuff IPMI, DHCP, DNS, PXE, etc.
